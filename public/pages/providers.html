Provider FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Fast answers for providers who want the practical details before they start sending scripts.

How long does it take to get onboarded?

Less than 5min. No, we’re not kidding. Yes, you can send in scripts that same day. We’re serious about efficiency because we know your practice demands it.

What if a patient needs something not in the catalog?

That’s literally why compounding pharmacies exist. We have a relationship with all our providers so that when their patients need something tailored, we can make it precisely.

What are standard turnaround times?

If you order a sterile compound product before 4pm CST, it ships same day. For non-sterile compounds, we’ll have it on it’s way in 24-48hrs.

Can I split between patient and clinic billing?

Absolutely, our portal allows you to choose the billing method with each order, so you can determine what’s right for your practice, patient by patient.
